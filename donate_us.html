What we do for people

Every year we provide winter cloth to those people who are economicaly backward in recent situation. Beside we donate books and other study equipments for distressed talented students. Along with we organise few social events such as Blood donation camp, Football Tournament and also sparkling concert during our major event "Saraswati puja".

We know it’s the right way to spread our new thought. For that reason from 2011 to 2019 we had been presenting exclusive Saraswati puja pandel. Aprat from that each year we tried to laid bare supreme resemblance to our goddess of knowledge and the arts; Devi Saraswati. We love to see when entire community gather together and create a friendly environment while enjoying our live concert event during Saraswati puja.
