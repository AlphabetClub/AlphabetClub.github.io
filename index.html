What we do for people

Every year we provide winter cloth to those people who are economically backward in recent situation. Beside we donate books and other study equipments for distressed talented students. Along with we organize few social events such as Blood donation camp, Football Tournament and also sparkling concert during our major event "Saraswati puja".


Some special moments of gifting study equipments to misrable people and a memorable curltural programme



We always ready to make creative things through each and every type of events. We have a passion for people, flair for ideas, and creative bravery that’s at the heart of everything we do. We know it’s the right way to spread our new thought. For that reason from 2011 to 2019 we had been presenting exclusive Saraswati puja pandel. Aprat from that each year we tried to laid bare supreme resemblance to our goddess of knowledge and the arts; Devi Saraswati. We love to see when entire community gather together and create a friendly environment while enjoying our live concert event during Saraswati puja.
